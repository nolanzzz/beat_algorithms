### Tree Traversals

- **Breadth First / Level Order**
  - 层次遍历/迭代
- **Depth First**
  - Pre-order前序遍历: _Root_, Left, Right
  - In-order中序遍历: Left, _Root_, Right (ascending order)
  - Post-order后序遍历: Left, Right, _Root_(leaves first, then root)

**Height:** bottom-up

 **Depth:** top-down
